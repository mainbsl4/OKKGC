    Safety Policy


    OKKGC gives safety a top priority in everyday activities. It is the policy of the company to provide and maintain a safety and health program consistent with the best practices of construction industries as well as those of our clients and host government.

    In fulfilling this commitment, which is essential and equally important as production objectives, the company provides and maintains a safe and healthy work environment, which protects the employees against possible hazards resulting from construction operations.
    The commitment to the safety program will be firm, aggressive and shall be the responsibility of all line management to ensure that all operations are performed with the utmost regard for the safety and health of all personnel under their direction.
    All levels of supervision shall be responsible for viable safety enforcement within their respective areas of accountability.

    It is the responsibility of every employee to support and promote the Safety and Health process in order to ensure a safe work environment consistent with the client requirements, Engineering Standards, Industry Practices and Saudi Arabian Government Regulations.
    All members of management and supervisors of all grades are well aware that safety must take equal importance with cost and construction and those they have a moral and legal responsibility to follow the standards and Saudi Arabian Law.
    These rules and practices are based on experience over many years.

    The Company Safety Officer and the full time Safety Advisors are responsible for achieving our goal of “NO ACCIDENTS” by conducting weekly safety meetings, to improve existing work methods.


    Health & Environmental Policy


    OKKGC is strongly and actively committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace and protecting the environment and communities that could be affected by our operations and project execution. We recognize that developing and engraining a responsible and robust health, safety and environmental program into our business are key to our success.

    The safety and wellbeing of employees, customers, subcontractors, suppliers and the communities we work and live in are paramount. No one will be expected, directed or encouraged to expose themselves or others to any uncontrolled risk in order to satisfy a work-related requirement.
    We comply with all pertinent environmental and safety laws, rules and regulations, not just because it is legally or contractually required, but also because we believe it is the responsible way to conduct our business.
